Job Title: Freelance Presentation Designer (PowerPoint & Infographics)

Job Description:

We are looking for a skilled Presentation Designer with Graphic design skills tomake a

responsible for designing graphic assets, infographics, and pictures to make the

presentation engaging, modern, and memorable. Our project covers approximately 15

slides, and it focuses on a revolutionary digital system for public administration. We are

providing a complete outline with the key information to be given per slides.

Responsibilities:

● Design and layout a 15-slide presentation based on our written outline and key

content.

● Create or source infographics, charts, icons, and illustrations that communicate

data clearly and effectively.

● Use high-quality stock images where needed, ensuring visuals are cohesive with

the overall design.

● Incorporate branding or thematic elements (if any) and maintain a consistent style

throughout.

● Collaborate with our team to revise and refine the slides until final approval.

Requirements:

● Proven experience designing professional, eye-catching presentations (PowerPoint

or a comparable tool).

● Strong graphic design skills, with a portfolio showcasing infographics, visuals, and

cohesive slide decks.

● Ability to conceptualize and implement a modern, sleek style that matches our

vision and brand tone.

● Knowledge of stock image platforms and best practices for image usage.

● Excellent communication and time management skills.

● Capacity to edit and iterate based on feedback promptly.

Budget & Terms:

● Rate is negotiable given that we are a small company with a tight budget.

● We are looking for a competitive proposal; please include your rate or typical fee

structure when applying.

● Successful collaboration may lead to more projects in the future (we anticipate

further design needs).

How to Apply:

● Send an email with the subject line “Freelance Presentation Designer – [Your

Name]”.

● Provide links or attachments to previous work (portfolio, website, or relevant

examples).

● Briefly describe how you approach presentation design and what tools you use.

● Include your availability, estimated timeline, and preferred payment structure.

We look forward to reviewing your work and finding a talented designer who can bring our

presentation to life! If you’re passionate about turning complex concepts into powerful

visuals, we'd love to hear from you.
